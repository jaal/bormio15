## Czarna Góra - Your Mountain Adventure

Czarna Góra is a picturesque region in the Sudetes, known for excellent skiing conditions, clean air, and beautiful landscapes year-round.

## About the Location

### Address

**57-512 Biała Woda**
**Poland**

Apartment Bormio 15 is located in the heart of this exceptional location, offering easy access to all attractions in the region.

## Skiing and Winter Sports

### Czarna Góra Ski Resort

Czarna Góra Resort is one of Poland's most modern ski resorts:

- **Ski slopes:** Over 15 km of slopes with varying difficulty levels
- **Lifts:** Modern gondolas and chairlifts
- **Artificial snow:** Guaranteed snow from December to March
- **Ski school:** Professional instructors for children and adults
- **Equipment rental:** Full range of ski and snowboard equipment

#### Slope Levels
- **Green slopes:** Ideal for beginners
- **Blue slopes:** Intermediate
- **Red slopes:** For experienced skiers
- **Black slopes:** For experts

### Distances from the Apartment
- 200m to the ski lift
- 200m to equipment rental
- 100m to pizzeria and café at the slope

## Clean Air

Czarna Góra is famous for **exceptionally clean air**:

### Air Quality Measurements
- **PM2.5:** Significantly below WHO standards
- **PM10:** Within ecological norms
- **No industry:** Area free from industrial pollution

Ideal place for:
- People with respiratory problems
- Families with small children
- Everyone appreciating a healthy lifestyle
