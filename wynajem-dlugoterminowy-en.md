## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I rent the apartment for 2 weeks?**
A: Our minimum is 1 month (30 days). For shorter stays, please make a standard reservation through [Sunandsnow.pl](https://www.sunandsnow.pl/apartament-panorama-aparthotelpanoramaczarnagoraresortsunsnow-15premiumbudynekbocznybormio-7620).

**Q: Can I extend the rental?**
A: Yes, provided the apartment is available for the next period. Contact us at least 2 weeks before the end of your current rental.

**Q: What happens if I use a lot of electricity?**
A: Standard consumption (up to 300 kWh/month) is included. If consumption is higher, additional kWh are charged according to actual usage.

**Q: Can I bring my dog/cat?**
A: We accept pets by prior arrangement, for an additional fee of 200 PLN/month.

**Q: Is the apartment available in winter?**
A: Yes! The apartment is available year-round. In winter, you additionally have access to the ski slope 200m from the apartment.

**Q: Do I have to clean myself?**
A: Yes, ongoing cleaning of the apartment is the tenant's responsibility. Optionally, we can arrange cleaning once a week for an additional fee.

**Q: What about breakdowns in the apartment?**
A: We provide quick contact and technical service in case of breakdowns. Contact number available 24/7.
