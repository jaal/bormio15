## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## General Information About the Apartment

### Where is the apartment located?
Apartment Bormio 15 is located at **Na Skarpie 10/15** in **Biała Woda** (57-512), in the Czarna Góra region in the Sudetes.

### How many people can stay in the apartment?
The apartment can accommodate **up to 4 people**:
- 2 people in the bedroom (two single beds)
- 2 people on the sofa bed in the living room

### Is there an elevator in the building?
Yes! The building is equipped with an elevator, making it easy to transport luggage and access the apartment on the first floor.

### Is smoking allowed in the apartment?
No, there is a **complete smoking ban** in the apartment.

### Can I bring pets?
Unfortunately, **we do not accept pets** in the apartment.

## Equipment and Amenities

### Does the apartment have WiFi?
Yes! Free WiFi is available throughout the apartment. Connection speed is **up to 40 Mbps**, which is sufficient for remote work, video conferences, and entertainment.

- Dishwasher

- Basic kitchen utensils

### Does the apartment have a balcony?
Yes! The apartment has a balcony with garden furniture and mountain views.

### Is there a TV?
Yes, there is a TV in the living room.

### Are towels and linens provided?
Yes, we provide a complete set of clean linens and towels for all guests.

## Parking and Access

### Is there parking available?
Yes, we offer **free parking** at the building.

### How far is it to the ski lift?
The ski lift is only **200 meters** from the apartment - about a 2-minute walk.

### How do I get to the apartment?
**By car:**
- From Wrocław: approx. 2 hours (130 km)
- From Warsaw: approx. 5 hours (450 km)
- From Krakow: approx. 4 hours (320 km)

**By public transport:**
- Bus to Stronie Śląskie, then local transfer
- Transfer can be arranged (ask when booking)
