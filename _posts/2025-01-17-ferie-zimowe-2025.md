Winter break 2025 is here, and mountain resorts across Poland are rolling out exceptional programming for families seeking snowy adventures. If you're planning a mountain getaway, here's everything you need to know about making the most of your winter holiday.

## Extended Operating Hours

Throughout the winter break period, Czarna Góra Resort maintains daily operations from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM, giving families ample time to enjoy all the mountain has to offer.

## Slope Entertainment Program

From Tuesday through Saturday, between 12:00 and 2:30 PM, the resort hosts engaging activities at the Efka cable car base station. These supervised sessions include:

- Dance-based warm-up activities to get everyone energized
- Challenging obstacle courses designed for various age groups
- Traditional winter games with creative props
- Delightful soap bubble competitions that enchant children and adults alike

## Ski & Snowboard Instruction

The resort's Skiing Academy provides professional group lessons tailored to young learners. Expert instructors focus on skill progression, ensuring that children build confidence and technique in a supportive environment.

## Promyczka Cup Racing Series

Every Friday during winter break, young skiers aged 6-15 can test their skills in the Promyczka Cup. These timed races offer:
- Age-appropriate courses
- Medals for all participants
- Podium recognition celebrating every competitor's achievement

## Ice Arena Adventures

The impressive 800-square-meter ice skating rink operates daily from noon until 9:00 PM. Families can enjoy:
- Equipment rental included in admission
- Specialized 5-day skating courses for skill development
- A scenic setting perfect for both beginners and experienced skaters

## Alpine Coaster Thrills

The Czarna Żmija (Black Viper) alpine coaster offers year-round excitement. This nearly one-kilometer track features:
- Speeds reaching approximately 40 km/h
- Three thrilling loops through the forest
- Scenic passages over streams and through woodlands
- Two-person carts perfect for family rides

## Accommodation Packages

Three partner hotels offer comprehensive packages including full meal plans, complimentary children's lodging, and ski passes - making family vacations both convenient and economical.

## Regional Schedule

Winter breaks are staggered across four periods from January 18 through February 28, accommodating different provincial school calendars throughout Poland. This distribution helps manage resort capacity and ensures quality experiences for all visitors.

Whether you're seeking adrenaline on the slopes, graceful glides on ice, or family bonding time over winter games, the 2025 winter break promises unforgettable mountain memories.
