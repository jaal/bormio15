## Dolní Morava — Mountains Full of Experiences

Dolní Morava is a family mountain resort that combines wild nature with modern attractions. Here, every day can be full of activities: from skiing and cross-country skiing in winter, to the chairlift, the Sky Bridge 721 viewing bridge, and the Mamut Mountain Coaster in summer.

At the resort, you'll find cycling trails, amusement parks for children, a bobsled track and tubing, as well as a rich selection of dining and wellness options.

### Main Attractions

- Sky Bridge 721 — an impressive viewing bridge over the valley.
- Mamut Mountain Coaster — thrills all year round.
- Ski and cross-country trails — conditions for beginners and advanced skiers.
- Bobsled track and tubing — entertainment for families and groups.
- Trail Park / single trails — cycling adventures for enduro enthusiasts.
- Wellness & spa — relaxation after a day full of impressions.

### Practical Information

- Tickets and passes: available online through the resort's official e-shop.
- Access and parking: details on the resort's website (maps and parking information).

If you're planning a family vacation or an active weekend in the mountains, Dolní Morava offers plenty of possibilities — from attractions for children to options for adrenaline and relaxation in the spa.
